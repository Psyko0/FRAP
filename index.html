Frap — Florent aupeix

Motion Designer & Title Designer


I build moving images that are sharp and emotive. My eye is shaped as much by cinema as by design schools: Villeneuve’s precise compositions, Burton’s playful madness, Nolan’s sense of scale, and Tarantino’s razor-edged rhythm.

For me, motion design is about rhythm: shaping beats so the audience knows exactly what to feel and when. Every project starts with the same question: what should the audience feel—and what’s the most direct way to show it?
