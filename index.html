FRAP is the portfolio site of Florent Aupeix.


I'm a motion designer with a Motion Design degree from MJM Design and a Graphic Design diploma from ESDAC (including a one-year apprenticeship).

I sharpened my eye at ArtFx (photo, sculpture, painting, drawing, film/VFX) and my 3D at Objectif3D, and I bring two years of computer science for pipeline-minded workflows.


I'm driven by cinema and video games—I tell clear, emotive stories.

On every brief I ask: what should the audience feel, and what's the most direct way to show it? Open to international collaborations—let's talk.
