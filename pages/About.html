What I do

  • Title design & typography in motion (main titles, interstitials, lower-thirds)
  • 3D-driven spots and graphic sequences (styleframes → animatics → final)
  • Hybrid workflows (Cinema 4D/Redshift ↔ After Effects) with clean comps and render discipline
  • Editorial clarity: shaping beats so the audience knows exactly what to feel and when

How I work

  1. Interrogate the brief → What should the audience feel? What’s the simplest way to show it?
  2. Prove it fast → styleframes + a rough timing test. No mystery reels.
  3. Design for constraints → GPU/RAM, deadlines, local render vs. farm.
  4. Name everything → tidy project structures, version control, handoff-ready files.
  5. Keep it non-destructive → layers, precomps, node-based setups: everything stays editable, because change always comes.

Tools

Cinema 4D + Redshift, After Effects, Photoshop/Illustrator, X-Particles when needed. Git-based versioning for assets, and a bias toward automation over manual fiddling.
